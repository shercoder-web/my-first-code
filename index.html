Ricardo Sousa @rickmixologist is a bar consultant for some of the best bars in Brazil. He has been practicing and teaching his skills to other bartenders for years. According to locals, he is currently the head mixologist in charge of creating, manipulating, training, and giving the best standard service according to locals! His work was also ranked number 10 in the World Class Competition 2021. In addition, he was also ranked number 10 on Best Margarita Cointreau Challenge worldwide 2021 and ranked the same spot on Flor de Cana Challenge 2020.
